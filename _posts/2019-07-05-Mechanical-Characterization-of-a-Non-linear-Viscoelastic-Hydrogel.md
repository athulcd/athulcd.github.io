I ideated and performed an indentation experiment using an Atomic Force Microscope (AFM) to determine the mechanical properties of a Poly Acryl Amide (PAA) viscoelastic hydrogel. The process also involved performing the corresponding Finite Element Method (FEM) simulations with the help of the experimental data using an axisymmetric model of the experimental setup in Abaqus FEA software. Then I tried to fit a constitutive model in ABAQUS software and calibrate the material parameters.
