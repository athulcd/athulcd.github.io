Comicbooks were among the fist books I ever read. I had a great collection of Amar-Chitra Katha, which introduced me to Hindu mythology, Indian history etc. As I grew up I was introduced to the more serious world of American comics. I started reading comics by publishers like Marvel, DC and authors/artists like Alan Moore, Chris Claremont, Neil Gaiman, Jack Kirby, Stan Lee, Grant Morrison etc
