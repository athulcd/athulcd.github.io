###### [Report](https://athulcd.github.io/assets/NLD_Report.pdf)   
Analysed various nonlinear contact model for use in computer simulation through literature survey. Chose a simple nonlinear contact model and studied it’s dynamics with the help of analytical methods as well as computer simulations and plots in MATLAB. Implemented the model in Simulink and used it to model the foot-ground interaction during locomotion cycle of a walking machine
