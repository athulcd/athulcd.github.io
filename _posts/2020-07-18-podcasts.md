Radio was always a marvelous gadget to me as a young child. I was fascinated by the fact that I can listen to BBC News on a simple hand held device. While I was in my high school I didn't have a smart phone or TV at home. Radio was my source of information as well as entertainment along with the newspaper. I used to listen to commentary of varios sporting competitions, classical music, news bulletins over radio.

While I was in college I had access to a wide range of resources for information and entertainment. But I still found audio as a medium very appealing. I noticed that podcasts have been gaining popularity over the last couple years and the number of podcasts have seen an exponential rise.
