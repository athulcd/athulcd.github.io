If you want to start watching and discovering classic/great movies review aggregators like Imdb, Rotten Tomatoes, Meta critic will be a good place to start with. As you progress and watch more movies you will realise that these metrics no longer accurately fit your taste. You can then start reading reviews by various critics(independent or associated to media bodies). Find someone whom you can connect with and explore his favorite movies.  
The academy of motion pictures(commonly referred as the Oscars), Golden Globes, BAFTA are some of the most prestigous awards. There are famous film festivals like Cannes, Berlin, Venice, Torronto which give out their own awards.  
If you want a curated list of greatest movies of all time the lists by AFI and BFI will be good place to start.
